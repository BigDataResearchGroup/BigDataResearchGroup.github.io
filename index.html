Welcome to Big Data Analytics Research Group

Harnessing Data to Drive Innovation

Introduction

The Big Data Analytics Research Group is an interdisciplinary team of researchers dedicated to harnessing the power of big data to tackle complex challenges and drive innovation across various domains, e.g., medical diagnosis & treatment, urban computing, finance, education, etc.

Research Areas

Our group has been working on a wide range of topics related to big data analytics, including:

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) data mining (e.g., clinical risk prediction and disease prediction)
  • Spatio-temporal (ST) data mining (e.g., traffic flow prediction)
  • Drug recommendation (e.g., Chinese herbal prescription recommendation)
  • Financial risk assessment
  • Students’ learning behaviors evaluation
  • Semantic analysis from massive unstructured text data (e.g., E-commerce product rating)

Our expertise lies in developing advanced data mining algorithms and leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence models to extract meaningful insights from massive and diverse datasets.

Group Leader

Wuman Luo

Dr. Wuman Luo is currently an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Applied Sciences of Macao Polytechnic University (MPU). She received the Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2013. Her research interests include Artificial Intelligence and Data Analysis in Big Data, Spatial and Temporal data mining, Urban Computing, Medial AI, and Parallel Computing. Her research works have been published in the international well-known conferences and journals, including ACM SIGMOD, IEEE ICDM, ACM CIKM, IEEE ICDCS, DASFAA, IEEE JBHI, Frontiers of Computer Science, KAIS, etc.
